South by Southwest

A twelve day, 5,081 mile roadtrip across the southwestern portion of the United States. Starting December 27th through January 7th, we trek across the country to take on life with a sense of wandering and adventure. From camping to couch surfing to crashing at friends of friends, we aim to keep money usage at a low and new life experiences at an all time high. The trip will be filled with unique and delicious food, great playlists (Red Rocks obvi), and quality life chats.
